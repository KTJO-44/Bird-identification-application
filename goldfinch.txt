GENERAL: It is found in
farmland with scattered woods
and plenty of rough, open ground.
Such places tend to be labelled
'waste' and are all too often
tidied up and stripped of the seed.
NESTING: Neat nest of roots,
grass and cobwebs, in tree or
shrub.
FEEDING: Soft, half-ripe
seeds on low-growing to medium-
height plants, less often on the
ground.
