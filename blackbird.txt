GENERAL: Ranging from remote
mountain forests to gardens and
parks, they are among the most
regular garden birds in many areas.
NESTING: Grass and mud cup, 
lined with grass, in shrub, bush, 
low in tree or hedge.
FEEDING: Worms, insects and
invertebrates on ground. Often
explore leaf litter. Fruits and
berries, scattered apples and 
bread.
