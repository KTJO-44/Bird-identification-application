GENERAL: The Whitethroat
likes overgrown tracksides,
railway embankments, hedgerows
and fence-lines with brambles, or
thorny thickets around heaths.
Often secretive, keeping low down
in thick vegetation.
NESTING: Small, neat cup of
grass and stems low in thorny
shrub.
FEEDING: Insects; lots of
berries and some seeds in late
summer and autumn.
