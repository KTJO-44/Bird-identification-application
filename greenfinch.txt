GENERAL: This large, stocky,
thick-billed finch breeds in
loose groups in tall trees, old
hedges, overgrown gardens and
orchards, and is a frequent
visitor in winter, either to feeders
or to shrubs with berries.
NESTING: Bulky nest of grass
and twigs, lined with finer stems,
hair and feathers in thick bushes
or trees.
FEEDING: Seeds, berries,
nuts, visits feeders.
