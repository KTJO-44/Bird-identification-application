GENERAL: Larger than a
Crow or Rook, with a longer tail.
Wedge or diamond shaped tail in
flight. Its heavier, longer bill
is more evident at close range.
NESTING: Huge nest of thick
sticks, wool, grass and heather.
Used for many years, under
overhang on cliff, or in tall tree.
FEEDING: Amost anything;
catches small mammals and birds.
Roadkill, invertebrates, grain.
