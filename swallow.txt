GENERAL: A summer visitor.
Of all the swallows and martins,
it has the most relaxed, easy
and flowing flight, often at the
lowest level while House Martins
feed higher up, almost as high as
the Swifts.
NESTING: Open-topped cup of
mud and straw, on beam or ledge
in outbuilding, shed or barn.
FEEDING: Flies low,
swerving to catch flying insects
in its mouth, mostly large flies.
