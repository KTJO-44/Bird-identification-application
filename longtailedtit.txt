GENERAL: Unique in its round-
bodied, long-tailed shape and
tiny size, the Long Tailed Tit
is usually encountered in groups.
Their high calls resemble those of
other tits and Goldcrests.
NESTING: Unique rounded and
domed nest with side entrance into
soft, springy, elastic ball of
lichen, moss, cobweb and feathers
in low, thorny bush.
FEEDING: Tiny spiders and
insects, seeds, visits feeders.
