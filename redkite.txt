GENERAL: Agility and
mastery in the air are
synonymous with the kites: the
Red Kite adds the appeal of colour
and pattern. More elegant than
the Buzzard, light, flexible and
has elasticity in its movements.
NESTING: Large nest of sticks,
rags, earth and rubbish in tree,
usually well-hidden.
FEEDING: Dead animals, such
as rabbits or sheep; also
insects, earthworms, voles.
