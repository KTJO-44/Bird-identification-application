GENERAL: In summer, it lives
around pools and reservoirs and
damp, grassy fields where horses
and cattle disturb the insects it
eats. In winter, it is found near
big mammal herds on African
plains.
NESTING: Grassy cup in
vegetation on ground.
FEEDING: Forages on ground,
skipping and leaping after flies
in short flycatching sallies; eats
insects and other invertebrates.
