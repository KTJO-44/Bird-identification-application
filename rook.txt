GENERAL: A big Crow,
very social, often mixed with
Jackdaws, Stock Doves and Black-
headed Gulls. It nests in
colonies in treetops. Identified
by white at base of beak.
NESTING: Big nest of sticks
in tree, lined with grass, moss
and leaves.
FEEDING: Worms, beetle
larvae, seeds, grain, and roots
from the ground.
